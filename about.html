About Our Project

Our Work

Our project is dedicated to helping first-year students understand the fundamentals of Web System Development. We have created a learning platform that offers well-organized lessons structured around to the topics covered in the ITWS (Information Technology Web System) course during the second semester. Each lesson is carefully written to ensure clarity, engagement, and practical relevance, making it easier for students to grasp even the most technical concepts. By focusing on accessibility and quality content, we aim to support learners in building a strong foundation in web development.

Purpose and Vision

The purpose of this project is to provide an effective educational resource that students can use at their own pace. Whether someone is just starting to learn HTML, our platform is structured to guide them through with clear explanations and structured lessons. We envision a platform that not only educates but also inspires students to explore and create using the knowledge they gain from our content.

Academic Context

This website was developed as part of our coursework for Web System Development, a subject offered in the second semester of the first year in the Information Technology program. The project reflects both our academic learning and our passion for making technology education more approachable and student friendly.

Tools and Platforms Used

To bring this project to life, we used several platforms and tools that helped us in content creation, coding, and testing:

  • Canva – for creating illustrations and visual content to enhance user engagement.
  • VS Code and SPCK Editor – used as our code editor and compiler for building and testing our web pages.
  • Google Chrome – for viewing and testing the website to ensure responsiveness and compatibility.
